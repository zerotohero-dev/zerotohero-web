Tools and trinkets.

Computer and Peripherals

  • Commodore 64: My first computer.
  • Mac Studio: I have a beefy Mac to do my work, content creation, gaming, and streaming.
  • Mac Mini Intel 2018: I use this as a local build and test environment, and experimental server.
  • Intel Nuc: I use this for other random experimentation. It has Ubuntu installed.
  • Logitech MX Master 3S: The fastest scroll ever; you’ll never realize how important scrolling is before you use this mouse. It’s very ergonomic too.
  • Logitech MX Keys: I use this keyboard mostly. It is silent, with a small form factor, and like MX Master 3, it can connect to up to 3 devices at the same time, which is very useful.
  • Das Keyboard 4 Professional: My default keyboard choice. I use this keyboard when I need to type a lot. It has Chery MX Blue keys, making it very clicky and loud, but it’s very satisfying.
  • CalDigit Thunderbolt Station: Adds 18 more ports to your machine for extreme connectivity.
  • InnoView Portable Monitor, 15.6 Inch: I use this as a secondary monitor for streams as a dedicated 1080p source.
  • Apple iPad Pro 4th Gen (11 inch): I use it for many things, including monitoring the alternate destinations when I stream to multiple platforms.
  • Anker 2.4G Wireless Vertical Ergonomic Optical Mouse: It’s very ergonomic; it’s a fraction of the price of MX Master 3, and it’s amazingly precise and comfortable. If you need an no-fluff ergonomic mouse, this is the one.

Home Office / Office

  • MP Trio Max: This gives two additional monitors to my laptop. It’s a great tool when you need more screen real estate. And it’s very portable too, which makes it a great fit for office and travel.
  • Crank Adjustable Height Rolling Standing Desk: I use this as my desk. It’s very sturdy, and it’s easy to adjust the height.
  • CyberPower Intelligent LCD UPS System: I use this to protect my computer and other devices from power outages.
  • Power Strip Tower and Surge Protector: I use this to power my devices.
  • TBMax Cat 6 Ethernet Cable 50 ft - High-Speed Flat Cat6 Computer LAN Wire: I use this to connect my computer to the router. I don’t believe in Wi-Fi.
  • Keyspan by Tripp Lite PR-PRO3 Presentation Remote Pro Wireless w Laser: I have a cat.
  • Rain Design 10032 mStand Laptop Stand, Silver: I use this to raise my laptop to eye level. You’ll never know when you need it.
  • Kogonee Wooden Digital Alarm Clock: You’ll never know you need this before you use it.

Storage

  • Transcend 256GB Thunderbolt Solid State Drive StoreJet 500 for Mac: I use this when I need a temporary high-speed SSD drive.
