Cloud Services Console

Cloud Services Console is VMware’s SaaS control plane for identity, organizations, subscriptions, and service access across VMware cloud offerings.

If vCenter answers “what is running?”, CSC answers:

“Who are you, what are you entitled to, and which VMware services can you access?”

Where CSC Sits

VMware SaaS Layer
└── Cloud Services Console (CSC)
    ├── Identity and Org management
    ├── Subscriptions & entitlements
    └── Access to VMware cloud services

On-prem / Private Cloud
└── VCF
    ├── vSphere / NSX / vSAN
    └── SDDC Manager

CSC:

  • Runs as: VMware-hosted SaaS
  • Audience: Org owners, platform leads, service-provider admins
  • Does not touch datapaths or place workloads

What Does CSC Do

Identity and Organization Management

  • Defines Organizations
  • Manages users, roles, and access
  • Integrates with enterprise IdPs (SSO)

This is business identity, not vCenter RBAC.

Subscription and Entitlement Visibility

  • Shows what your org is entitled to:
    • VCF subscriptions
    • Optional VMware services
  • Central place to view contracts → services

CSC does not meter usage—that’s elsewhere.

Entry point to VMware cloud services

From CSC you access:

  • Support portals
  • VMware cloud SaaS
  • Management consoles (where applicable)

CSC is the front door, not the engine room.

CSC vs Similarly-Named Consoles

Console Purpose
Cloud Services Console (CSC) Global identity and Entitlement (SaaS)
Business Services Console (BSC) VCF business governance and usage
vCenter Infrastructure management
SDDC Manager VCF lifecycle and config

CSC ≠ BSC
CSC is VMware-global; BSC is VCF-specific.

How Does CSC and VCF Relate

  • CSC knows about your subscriptions
  • VCF consumes those entitlements
  • CSC does not:
    • Provision domains
    • Run LCM
    • Enforce quotas
    • Observe performance

That work stays on-prem with VCF components.

What CSC is not

  • Not part of the VCF runtime
  • Not an ops or automation tool
  • Not a billing/metering engine
  • Not a tenant boundary for workloads

If VCF is:

“Your private cloud platform”

Then CSC is:

“Your VMware account and service access hub”

Summary

  • Cloud Services Console = SaaS identity and entitlement layer
  • Global across VMware services
  • Adjacent to VCF, not embedded in it
  • Often confused with VCF Business Services Console—they serve different roles
